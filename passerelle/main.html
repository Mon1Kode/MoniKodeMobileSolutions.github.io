About La Passerelle 🧑‍💻

La Passerelle stands out as an innovative support program, specially designed for individuals awaiting care in a specialized service.

Description 🎨

  • ACTIVITIES Activities such as talking circles, sophrology sessions and emotion management workshops are organized to equip participants with skills essential to their well-being. 🌐
  • RESOURCES Consult a wide range of books and websites on various pathologies. 🍏
  • TOOLS Organize and prepare your appointments with the ToDo list and customizable calendar. 🤖
